Privacy Policy

Property Inspections attaches great importance to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains which data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

1. What data do we collect?

We only collect data you provide to us, such as name, phone number, email address, and address, when you contact us or schedule an appointment.

2. Why do we use this data?

  • To contact you and schedule appointments.
  • To carry out our services properly.
  • To provide invoices and related documents.

3. Sharing data

Your data will not be sold or shared with third parties, except where required by law.

4. Retention period

Your data is retained only as long as necessary for providing our services and in accordance with legal obligations.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Please contact us via contact@pibelgium.com.

6. Additional rights

In addition to access, correction, and deletion, you also have the right to object to processing, request processing restrictions, or receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

7. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against loss, misuse, or unauthorized access.

8. Complaints

If you have questions or are dissatisfied with how we handle your data, please contact us via contact@pibelgium.com. If your rights are not respected, you can also file a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority (GBA) at www.gegevensbeschermingsautoriteit.be.
