Privacy Policy

1. Processing of personal data

Property Inspections attaches great importance to protecting your privacy. We process personal data only in accordance with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

2. What data do we collect?

  • Identification data (name, address, email, phone number)
  • Property details for inspection
  • Billing and payment data

3. Purposes of processing

Your data is used for conducting property inspections, invoicing, and communication with clients.

4. Sharing of data

Your data will not be shared with third parties, unless legally required or necessary for the performance of our services.

5. Retention period

We do not keep your data longer than necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

6. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and transfer your data. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at contact@pibelgium.com.

7. Complaints

If you have a complaint, you may contact the Data Protection Authority (DPA) via www.dataprotectionauthority.be.
