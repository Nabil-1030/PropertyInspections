Property inspections across Belgium

A property report is an official document recording the condition of a property at move-in or move-out. Each room, wall, floor, door, window, and installation is described in detail, often with supporting photos.

Move-in report

Records the complete condition of the property at the start of the rental agreement.

Move-out report

Compared with the move-in report to objectively determine any damages.

Legal framework (Belgium)

A property report is legally required and must be contradictory: both parties must have the opportunity to be present and provide feedback.

  • Legal basis: Belgian Civil Code, rental law.
  • Contradictory nature: required for legal validity.
  • Purpose: protects both tenant and landlord.
