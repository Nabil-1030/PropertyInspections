Payment information and conditions

Our rates are per person and apply to each inspection we carry out. This means that both the landlord and the tenant can be responsible for payment, unless otherwise agreed in advance.

The purpose of this arrangement is to guarantee an objective and independent inspection, with both parties involved in the process. For example:

  • Apartment 2 bedrooms: €185 excl. VAT / €223.85 incl. VAT per person
  • House with garden: €250 excl. VAT / €302.50 incl. VAT per person

In practice, the landlord may take full responsibility for the payment, or the tenant and landlord may share the costs. If another arrangement is agreed between the parties beforehand, we will of course adapt accordingly.

Important: The inspection is only considered final and legally binding once the payment has been correctly received. This ensures that all parties have certainty regarding the validity of the document.

We aim to ensure that all agreements are transparent, professional, and legally correct, so you can fully rely on our reports.
